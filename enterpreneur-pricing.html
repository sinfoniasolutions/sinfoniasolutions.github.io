IT Support for Enterpreneurs

Learn about Our Pricing for Enterpreneurs and Startups

Don't forget, even enterpreneurs get 30 days free.

When you're a startup, high overhead can destroy your business.

But you still need quality IT support so you can focus on what really matters. That's where we come in.

Sinfonia Solutions takes 2-10% of your gross revenue for the lifetime of our contract with you, plus an additional 5 years. That means that we have an incentive to help you succeed, and we only make money when you make money.

What's the difference between 2% and 10%?

It's a judgement call on our part. The riskier the startup, the greater the percentage. We want a greater reward for shouldering more risk.

We also look at your organization size and typical revenue for the industry, and adjust the percentage so that we're likely to make a profit if your business succeeds. It's important for our success to be linked to your success. Otherwise, we won't be doing our best work, and you deserve our best.
